Pattern Configuration 
	[id_user,domain,subdomain,skill,performance,language, typeQ]

Ranges
	id_user: 00000-99999 - [id_user: 1 - Gil, 2 - Diana, 3 - Luis, 4 - Raul, 5 - Kiko] 5 users
	domain: 00-99 - [domain: 1 - Base Dados, 2 - Biologia, 3 - Fisica, 4 - Informatica, 5 - Enfermagem] 5 domains
	subdomain: 00-99 - [subdomain: BD - 1,2,3 ; Bio - 1,2,3 ; Fis - 1,2,3 ;  Info - 1,2,3 ; Enf - 1,2,3] 15 subdomains
	skill: 00-99 - [skill: TERRIBLE 0-20, BAD 20-40, AVERAGE 40-60, GOOD 60-80, EXCELLENT 80-99 ]
	performance: 00-99 [performance: TERRIBLE 0-20, BAD 20-40, AVERAGE 40-60, GOOD 60-80, EXCELLENT 80-99 ]
	language: 0-9 [language 1-PT, 2-UK, 3-ES, 4-DE, 5-FR, 6-RU] 6 languages
	typeQ: 00-99 [greetingsI-1, greetingsA-2, domain-3, subdomain-4, doubt-5, bye-6, timeout-7]

Example 
	[4,3,12,85,57,3,1]
